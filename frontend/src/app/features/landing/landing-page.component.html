How Sendora Works

Send your parcel in 5 simple steps. Fast. Reliable. Transparent.

1. Register

Sign up and create your Sendora account with your contact details.

2. Present Parcel

Visit any of our nearest drop-off points. Our agent will record your parcel info and prep it for delivery.

3. Confirmation

Receive an email with tracking info and parcel receipt details.

4. Transport

Your parcel is shipped using our secure and fast delivery network.

5. Delivered

Parcel reaches the recipient safely. We notify you once delivery is complete.
